Becoming is the memoir of former First Lady of the United States Michelle Obama, published on 2018. Described by the author as a deeply personal experience, the book talks about her roots and how she found her voice, as well as her time in White House, her public health compaign, and her role as a mother. The book is published by Crown and was released in 24 languages. One million copies were donated to First Book, an American nonprofit organization which provides books to children.
